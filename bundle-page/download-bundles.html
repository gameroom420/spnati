SPNATI Bundle Downloads

From this page, you can download SPNATI's Offline Version, containing everything in the Online Version (at spnati.net) as well as older archived opponents.

To get the Offline Version:

  1. Download any combination of ZIP bundles below.
  2. Extract every downloaded bundle to the same directory. Their contents should overlap.
